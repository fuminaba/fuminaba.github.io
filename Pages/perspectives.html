Perspectives on Digitization of Medical Care and Research

Development of computational technologies have led to digitization of various aspects medicine, leading to increased efficiency and productivity in areas like healthcare and research. The emergence of AI technologies in particular have made extremely complex analyses of data and patterns possible. This has led to a boom in utilizing various machine learning and deep learning models in medical research, making data science an increasingly popular field in medical research.
Although many data science tools, such as deep learning models, can be extremely effective tools for analysis and prediction, they are prone to problems such as overfitting, bias, etc. Many researchers are starting to voice concerns of a reproducibility crisis, where results of a machine/deep learning model do not generalize to new/external data.
